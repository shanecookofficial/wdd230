About Us

Founded in 1984 in Carlsbad, California, Bountiful Foods has grown from a small family-run farm stand to a beloved local institution. Our mission is simple yet profound: to provide fresh, healthy, and organic food options to our community. We pride ourselves on sourcing directly from local farmers, supplemented by select national and international suppliers, ensuring a diverse and high-quality selection for our customers.

Whether you visit our Carlsbad storefront or encounter one of our food trucks along the California coast, you'll find a range of fresh produce, fruit drinks, and organic delights. Our services extend from walk-in traffic to convenient phone and website ordering, catering to both the local population and tourists. At Bountiful Foods, every bite is a step towards a healthier, more sustainable lifestyle.
