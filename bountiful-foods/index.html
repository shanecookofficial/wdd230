Carlsbad State Beach

Carlsbad State Beach, located in Southern California, is a popular destination known for its golden sands and scenic ocean views.

It offers a range of activities such as swimming, surfing, and sunbathing, along with convenient amenities for visitors. This beach is ideal for family outings or simply enjoying the coastal ambiance of Carlsbad.
