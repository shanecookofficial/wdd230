Resources

I have thoroughly enjoyed working on this project. I spent the past several weeks putting my heart and soul into this project and I couldn't have done it without the resources I compiled to complete this site.

Images

Bountiful Foods Logo

I am extremely proud of this logo. When I was designing a logo for Bountiful Foods, I wanted to have a logo that held a strong grasp on the idea of organic, locally sourced foods. I was able to come up with several versions at the beginning and settled on the horizon and rolling California hills up top and a pleathora of fresh grown foods at the bottom.

Home Page Hero Image

This image is a photo taken by Alex Raths in Germany. When I was looking for hero images to really make my home page pop, this one stuck out more than all of the others. Thank you Alex for creating an absolutely stunning image. If you are interested in viewing the image or checking out more of Alex's work, click this link.

Carlsbad State Beach Image

I feel like this image of the Carlsbad State beach really brings a warm homey feel to the site. I was unable to find the original capture of this image but I do want to thank Wavehuggers for including it on their site.
