Membership Benefits

NP Membership (Non-Profit)

  • No membership fee
  • Access to monthly networking events
  • Basic listing in the online directory

Bronze Membership

  • Annual fee: $100
  • Includes NP benefits
  • Access to specialized training sessions
  • Spotlight feature in the monthly newsletter

Silver Membership

  • Annual fee: $250
  • Includes Bronze benefits
  • Discounts on event sponsorships
  • Priority access to chamber events
  • Enhanced listing in the online directory

Gold Membership

  • Annual fee: $500
  • Includes Silver benefits
  • Free advertising space on the chamber website
  • Exclusive invitations to VIP events
  • Opportunity to host events or workshops
