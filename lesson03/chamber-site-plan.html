Site Name

Rexburg Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The Rexburg Chamber of Commerce serves as a driving force for regional economic growth, a unifying platform for leadership, and a dedicated advocate for the community. Our mission encompasses advancing business interests through member-focused services, fostering economic and community development, and advocating for public policies that harmonize economic well-being.

Scenarios

Scenario #1: A local business owner or entrepreneur is looking to expand their network and access resources to grow their business in Rexburg.

Q1: "Where can I find information on networking events, business workshops, and resources available for local businesses in Rexburg?"

Scenario #2: An out-of-town visitor is planning a trip to Rexburg and wants to explore the area, find accommodations, and discover local attractions.

Q2: "What are the best places to stay, dine, and explore in Rexburg? Can I get a visitor's guide or map of the area?"

Scenario #3: A resident of Rexburg is interested in getting involved in community events, volunteering opportunities, or staying informed about local happenings.

Q3: "How can I participate in community events, volunteer in Rexburg, or stay updated on local news and initiatives?"

Color Schema

Role Color Usage
Primary Color #8ECAE6 (Light Blue) This will be the dominant color used for various elements like the website's header, main navigation, and call-to-action buttons.
Secondary Color #219EBC (Dark Blue) This color will be for secondary elements like subheadings, borders, and other design accents.
Accent Color #FFB703 (Yellow) The accent color will be used for highlighting important information, buttons, and interactive elements.
Complimentary Color #FB8500 (Orange) This color will be used sparingly for specific elements that need to stand out, such as important calls to action or promotions.
Text Colors #000000 (Black) and #FFFFFF (White) Black will go on any light backgrounds and white will go on any dark backgrounds.
